AI Customer Care Ticket Classification & Resolution Prediction

Business Problem

Customer service teams receive large volumes of support tickets, causing delays and inconsistent response times.

AI Solution

Implemented an AI model to automatically classify customer service tickets and predict resolution time.

Key Features

  • Automatic ticket classification
  • Priority prediction
  • Resolution time prediction
  • Service efficiency monitoring

Tools Used

  • Natural Language Processing (NLP)
  • Machine Learning
  • Power BI
  • SQL
  • Python

Business Impact

Reduced response time, improved customer satisfaction, and increased operational efficiency.
