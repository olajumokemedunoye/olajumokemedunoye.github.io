AI Workforce Planning & Attrition Prediction

Business Problem

Organisations struggle to predict employee turnover and workforce shortages, leading to staffing gaps and increased recruitment costs.

AI Solution

Developed a predictive workforce planning model using historical employee data to identify employees at risk of leaving and forecast staffing needs.

Key Features

  • Predict employee attrition risk
  • Forecast workforce demand
  • Identify high-risk departments
  • Support workforce planning decisions

Tools Used

  • Python
  • Machine Learning
  • Power BI
  • SQL
  • Excel

Business Impact

Enabled proactive workforce planning, reduced staffing shortages, and improved employee retention strategies.
